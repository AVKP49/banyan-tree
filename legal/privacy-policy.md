# Privacy Policy — The Banyan Tree

**Last updated:** May 2026

The Banyan Tree ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our service.

## What We Collect

**From Children:** Nothing. We do not collect personal information from children. Voice input is processed entirely on the child's device using the browser's built-in speech recognition; no audio recordings are transmitted to or stored on our servers.

**From Parents (Phase 1+):** Email address for account creation, and an optional display name. Payment information is processed by Stripe and is never stored on our servers.

**From All Users:** Anonymous, non-identifying usage analytics (page views, feature usage rates) via privacy-respecting analytics tools that use no cookies and collect no personal data.

## What We Do NOT Collect

- Audio recordings of children's voices
- Voice biometrics
- Precise location data
- Advertising identifiers
- Social media profiles
- Contact lists

## How We Use Data

- Questions asked to the "Ask Dadi" feature are processed in real-time to generate responses and are logged as text only (never audio) for the parent's review in the Parent Zone
- Anonymous usage data helps us understand which stories are popular and how the product is used
- Parent email (Phase 1+) is used only for account access and transactional communications

## Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on secure servers (Cloudflare, Supabase) with encryption at rest and in transit. API keys and secrets are never exposed in client-side code.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The Ask Dadi feature is designed so that no personal data leaves the child's device except the text of their question.

## Your Rights

- **Access:** You can view all data associated with your device in the Parent Zone
- **Deletion:** You can delete all data using the "Reset device" button in the Parent Zone, or by contacting us
- **Portability:** We will provide your data in a portable format upon request
